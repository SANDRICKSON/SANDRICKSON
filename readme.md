## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a results-oriented **Full-Stack Developer** based in Tbilisi, Georgia, with a passion for crafting robust and scalable digital solutions. My expertise spans the entire development lifecycle, from conceptualizing intuitive mobile apps with **Flutter** to building powerful backends with **Python** and integrating cutting-edge **AI** technologies.

I thrive on solving complex problems and am constantly exploring new frontiers in tech. Whether it's optimizing a database query or designing a seamless user interface, I'm committed to writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code.
